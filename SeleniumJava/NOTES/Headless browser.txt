Headless browsers can be used for:
Running your test on a headless systems like Unix or without UI.
When you want simulate multiple browser behavior on a single machine.
To use minimal resources (because there is no UI)
Need to run your test just for data creation.


Should not be used:
If you need to visually observe your test on the browser.
It does not give you the exact feedback of what your customers will actually experience on your website (Page loading speed)
Headless browsers sometimes may throw errors that is not observed on real browser.


HTMLUnit driver can be executed only on WebDriver and cant be run on the Selenium RC
